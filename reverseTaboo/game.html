Thing Explainer Reverse-Taboo Game

This is a word game based on Taboo and inspired by "Thing Explainer", by Randall Munroe of the comic XKCD. The cluer (Person 1) is given a word that they must clue, and everyone else must guess the word based on the clue. The catch -- the cluer can only use words that are among the most common words used in English!

Tips for the cluer:

  • Click "Check your clue" to remove not-allowed words.
  • Numbers and punctuation will be ignored. Type name of numbers (e.g. "one" instead of "1").
